NFPA 10 Standard

What Does Compliance Actually Require?

NFPA 10 is the standard for portable fire extinguishers. It requires documented inspections, maintenance records, and proper placement tracking. FireWatch handles all of it.

  • Monthly visual inspections with documented records
  • Annual maintenance inspections by qualified personnel
  • Proper extinguisher placement by occupancy type
  • Hydrostatic testing at required intervals
  • Immediate corrective action on failed units
  • Complete maintenance history per extinguisher
